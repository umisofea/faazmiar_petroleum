Our Story

Founded in 1995, Faazmiar Petroleum has grown from a small regional operator to an international energy company with operations across Southeast Asia.

Our journey began with a single refinery in Malaysia and has expanded to include exploration, production, and distribution activities across the region. Today, we're recognized as a leader in implementing sustainable practices within the petroleum industry.

We pride ourselves on our commitment to innovation, safety, and environmental stewardship while meeting the world's growing energy needs.

Our Mission

To responsibly meet the world's energy needs by exploring for, producing, and delivering petroleum products in innovative and sustainable ways.

We are committed to operational excellence, technological innovation, and the highest standards of safety and environmental stewardship.

Our Vision

To be the leading petroleum company in Southeast Asia, recognized for excellence in sustainable energy development and community engagement.

We aim to drive the transition to a lower-carbon future while continuing to provide reliable energy that powers economic growth and improves quality of life.
